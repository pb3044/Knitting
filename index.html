About Our Mission

Since September 2010, we've been bridging communities across continents, connecting talented women artisans from the rural Himalayan region of Manali with opportunities in Victoria, British Columbia.

Bimla Creations is built on the belief that traditional crafts can create sustainable livelihoods while preserving cultural heritage. Every hand-knit toque and scarf tells a story of skill, dedication, and community support.

Our model ensures 100% of proceeds return directly to the artisans, strengthening income stability and building community resilience in the Himalayas. Items are sold locally in Victoria or donated to women's shelters, creating impact on both sides of the world.

Our Approach

A four-step model for sustainable artisan empowerment

1

Identify & Connect

Building partnerships with skilled women artisans in rural Himalayan communities

2

Support Production

Providing resources, training, and ongoing support for high-quality craftsmanship

3

Distribute & Sell

Organizing shipping and local distribution in Victoria, BC markets

4

Reinvest & Scale

Returning 100% of proceeds to artisans, ensuring sustainable growth

Our Programs

Creating opportunities through traditional crafts

Artisan Production

Supporting 15 women artisans in Manali to create beautiful hand-knit toques and scarves using traditional Himalayan techniques and high-quality materials.

  • Traditional patterns preserved
  • Quality craftsmanship guaranteed
  • Fair wages for all work

Local Distribution

Coordinating shipping from Manali to Victoria, BC and selling items through local markets and community events.

  • Direct market access
  • Community engagement
  • Transparent pricing

Community Giving

Donating items to women's shelters in Victoria, providing warmth and comfort to those in need while supporting artisans.

  • Shelter partnerships
  • Meaningful donations
  • Dual-community impact
